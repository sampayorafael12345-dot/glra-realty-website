Meet Catherine

10+ Years of Trusted Real Estate Expertise

Catherine SB Sampayo is the licensed broker behind GLRA Realty. With more than 10 years of hands-on experience in the Philippine property market, she has helped hundreds of clients — from first-time homebuyers to seasoned investors — find the right property at the right price.

Her approach is simple: full transparency, deep market knowledge, and a relentless focus on the client's goals. No pressure tactics, no shortcuts — just honest guidance from start to finish.

Based in Makati and serving all of Metro Manila and Luzon, Catherine specializes in residential condos, house and lot, commercial leases, and investment properties.
